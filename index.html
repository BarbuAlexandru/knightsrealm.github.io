Welcome to the realm

A tiny kingdom. A big goblin problem.

Knight's Realm is a cozy-chaotic real-time strategy game. Chop wood, herd sheep, mine gold and build your stronghold — then defend every last plank of it from goblins armed with torches, exploding barrels and questionable judgement.

VS

How you'll spend your reign

Gather. Build. Recruit. Defend.

Gather & prosper

Send your pawns to fell forests, herd sheep and crack gold ore. Every log, chop and coin is hauled home to your castle by hand.

Build your stronghold

Place houses, barracks, archeries, monasteries and towers. Pawns hammer each construction site together, plank by plank.

Recruit your army

Train lancers, archers, monks and generals — each with their own fighting style, temperament and favourite way to bonk a goblin.

Defend the realm

Garrison archers in towers and hold the walls. Goblins raid with torches, rolling powder barrels and thrown TNT — stay sharp.

Your loyal subjects

Meet the knights

Four banners fly over the realm. Pick your colours — every knight (and every rooftop) follows suit.

Frontline

General

Your sword-and-shield backbone. Points at goblins; goblins stop existing.

Reach

Ranged

Archer

Support

Monk

Patches up wounded knights with a sparkle and a stern look.

Worker

Pawn

The tireless heart of your economy. Chops, mines, hauls and hammers.

The green menace

Know your goblins

They live in huts, multiply fast, and have exactly one hobby: ruining your afternoon.

Arsonist

Torch Goblin

Runs at your buildings waving fire. Your wooden buildings. Sets them ablaze, cackles, repeats.

Ambusher

Barrel Goblin

Hides inside a TNT barrel, peeks out to pick a victim… then charges. You can guess how that ends.

Artillery

TNT Goblin

Lobs lit dynamite from a safe distance — and climbs goblin towers to lob it even further.

From campfire to castle

Build your realm

Every building starts as a construction site and gets hammered together by your pawns — and each one you add makes the next a little pricier, so plan your village wisely.

Castle

The heart of your realm and drop-off for every resource. Lose it, and the goblins win the day.

A world that lives without you

Every island is one of a kind

Each new game generates a fresh island — beaches, cliffs, forests and gold veins, with four rival castles staking their corners. Sheep graze and multiply, forests regrow, mines run deep… and goblin camps fester in the wilds.
