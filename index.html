Welcome to the realm

A tiny kingdom. A big goblin problem.

A cozy-chaotic real-time strategy game about raising a village from nothing — and keeping it standing when the horde comes knocking.

01

Gather & build

Work the land, stockpile what you need and raise a stronghold that fits the island you were given.

02

Command your knights

Recruit and lead an army with real roles to play — every order you give costs you something somewhere else.

03

Hold the line

The goblins come at night, in numbers, and they are not subtle about it. Be ready, or be rebuilding.
