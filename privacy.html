Privacy Policy

The short version: this site doesn't collect anything about you. Really.

Last updated: July 17, 2026

What this site collects

Nothing. This website:

  • sets no cookies;
  • runs no analytics or tracking scripts;
  • uses no third-party fonts, CDNs or embeds — everything is served from this site itself;
  • collects, stores and processes no personal data.

There are no accounts, no forms and no newsletter. If you press play on a soundtrack, the audio plays in your browser and that's the end of the story.

Hosting

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. Like virtually all web hosts, GitHub may log basic technical information about requests (such as IP addresses) for security and operational purposes. That logging is governed by GitHub's privacy statement; we never see or receive that data.

External links

The site links out to third-party services — such as Steam, Google Play, the App Store, Instagram and YouTube. Once you follow such a link, the privacy policy of that service applies. We recommend reviewing their policies if you have concerns.

The game itself

This policy covers the website only. If Realm of Knights: The Siege (the game) ever collects any data — for example, crash reports or cloud saves — it will ship with its own clear privacy policy, and this page will link to it.

Fonts & licenses

Headings on this site use the MedievalSharp typeface, self-hosted and licensed under the SIL Open Font License. No font requests leave this site.

Changes to this policy

If anything above ever changes, this page will be updated and the "last updated" date revised. Given how little there is to change, don't expect much drama.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Write to contact@knightsrealm.example.
